About Us


  1. Kishinchand Chellaram College, commonly known as KC College, is a higher educational Autonomous institution in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

  2. It is affiliated to the HSNC University.It was the first college to be awarded an "A" grade by The National Assessment and Accreditation Council in Mumbai.

  3. KC College was established in 1954 under the aegis of the Hyderabad (Sind) National Collegiate Board.

  4. The college has been re-accredited with an A grade by NAAC in three consecutive cycles. and adjudged the Best College (Urban Area) by the University of Mumbai in 2015.In 2019, the college was awarded Star College Status and Star Scheme by the Dept. of Biotechnology, Govt. of India.

  5. The college is also funded with the prestigious FIST program by DST (Department of Science and Technology) Govt. of India.
